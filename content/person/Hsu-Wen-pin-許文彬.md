# 許文彬 (Hsu Wen-pin)

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

| **English Name** | Hsu Wen-pin |
| **Profession** | Lawyer (律師) |
| **Role** | Community leader, human rights advocate |

## Career

### Law Practice
**許文彬律師事務所** (Law Firm of Hsu Wun-Pin)
- Founded: **March 23, 1976** (ROC year 65)
- Location: 台北市中正區仁愛路二段25號3樓, Taipei
- Location noted for being in the "博愛特區" (Bo'ai Special Zone) alongside all levels of courts

### Human Rights Advocacy
**Chinese Association for Human Rights (CAHR)**
- Founded and served as President
- CAHR dispatched volunteer teams to assist indigenous communities
- Efforts focused on encouraging young Aborigines to take action on human rights issues
- Reported in Taipei Times (2004) on volunteer dispatch efforts

## Community Connections

- Early supporter of Pastor Chang's pioneering church work in the Los Angeles Taiwanese American community
- Listed among: 高光民、陳昭俊、葉思雅、李宗派、王春雄、吳政彥、賴信雄
- Also mentioned in the Encyclopedia Taiwanese American (Our Journeys) as a second-term president of the Community Federation (社團聯合會) in 1983
