# 高光民 Gao Guangmin

## Biographical Information

**Names:** 高光民 (Hanzi) / Gao Guangmin (Pinyin)

## Role in the Community

高光民 was listed as one of the founding supporters of the organization/Formosan-Presbyterian-Church-Los-Angeles-FPCLA (Formosa Presbyterian Church, Los Angeles). He is recognized as one of the early community founders in the Taiwanese American community of Los Angeles.
