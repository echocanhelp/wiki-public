# 葉思雅 (Sze-ya Yeh)

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Chinese Name** | 葉思雅 |
| **English Name** | Sze-ya Yeh |
| **Birth Year** | 1937 |
| **Birth Place** | 台北, Taiwan |
| **Title** | Professor Emeritus, M.D. |
| **Field** | OB/GYN, Perinatal Medicine |

## Education

- **National Taiwan University** — M.D., 1962
- **Yale University** — Postdoctoral Fellowship, 1969

## Medical Career

- Professor of OB/GYN at multiple Philadelphia institutions: Temple, Hahnemann, Albert Einstein Medical Center, Jefferson Medical College
- **First recipient** of the "Society of Perinatal Obstetricians Achievement Award" (1994)
- Chairman Emeritus, Albert Einstein Medical Center (2002)

## Community Roles

### Formosan Presbyterian Church in Los Angeles (FPCLA)
- **Co-founder** and one of the first elders
- First choir director (1971–1974)

### Taiwanese-American Religious Study Association (TARSA-SC)
- **Founder and President**, established October 29, 2009
- Founded after conservative elements at FPCLA declared interfaith dialogue "heresy" and blocked lectures
- Resigned collectively with the Evergreen Fellowship (長青團契), then established TARSA to continue cross-religious dialogue
- Meets monthly at the LA Taiwanese Cultural Association (大洛杉磯台灣會館)

### Family Connection
- Married to Grace 張信惠, daughter of **張逢昌牧師 (Rev. Chang)** — making him Pastor Chang's son-in-law
- Early supporter of Pastor Chang's pioneering church work in the Los Angeles Taiwanese American community

### Recognition
- **Elected Outstanding Taiwanese American** by T.A. Archives, August 2018
