# Rev. Frank Hsieh (謝信光牧師)

**Rev. Frank Hsieh** (謝信光牧師) is a Taiwanese American pastor currently serving as the pastor of [[organization/First-Taiwanese-Presbyterian-Church|First Taiwanese Presbyterian Church]] (第一台灣基督長老教會) in Pasadena, California, and as Secretary of the [[organization/NTPC]] (美國基督長老教會全國台灣人教會聯合會).

- **Romanized Name:** Frank Hsieh
- **Current Position:** Pastor, [[organization/First-Taiwanese-Presbyterian-Church|First Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (FTPC)]]
- **NTPC Role:** Secretary (書記), TPC Chair

## Ministry

### First Taiwanese Presbyterian Church

Rev. Hsieh pastors [[organization/First-Taiwanese-Presbyterian-Church|First Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (FTPC)]] in Pasadena, which serves the Taiwanese American community in the [[location/San-Gabriel-Valley-聖蓋博谷|San Gabriel Valley]] with worship services in [[Taiwanese language|台語]], Mandarin, and English.

### NTPC Secretary

As Secretary of the [[organization/NTPC]], Rev. Hsieh coordinates communication among 16 member Taiwanese Presbyterian churches across the United States, manages annual conference logistics, and serves on the NTPC Executive Committee representing the Southwest Region.
