## Biography

Dr. Bob Cheng was born in **Tainan, Taiwan** in 1940. He arrived in the United States in **1965**, settling in Syracuse, New York. He worked as a chemist and entrepreneur, building a successful career before dedicating himself to documenting Taiwanese American history.

### T.A. Archives (台美史料中心)
Dr. Cheng founded the **T.A. Archives** in 2013, initiating the project "The History of Taiwanese Americans in the Last Six (6) Decades." He compiled photo albums and historical archives over two decades before his passing in 2020.

The archive at **taiwaneseamericanhistory.org** now contains **2,227+ Taiwanese American profiles**, a comprehensive encyclopedia (Our Journeys), extensive photo albums, and archival documents — all driven by his vision of preserving and promoting Taiwanese American heritage.

### Legacy
Dr. Cheng's work has become one of the most important resources for Taiwanese American history and community documentation. His archive is also cataloged by the **University of California system** via Calisphere.
