# Rev. Ming Yuan Hsu (許明遠牧師)

**Rev. Ming Yuan Hsu** (許明遠牧師) is a Taiwanese American pastor currently serving as the full-time pastor of Good Shepherd Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (好牧者臺灣基督長老教會) in Monterey Park, California.

## Basic Information

- **Chinese Name:** 許明遠
- **Romanized Name:** Ming Yuan Hsu
- **Birthplace:** [[location/Tainan-台南]] (台南), Taiwan
- **Spouse:** Chou Mei-Ling (周美玲), married 1990
- **Ordination:** Minister of Word and Sacrament, Los Ranchos Presbytery, [[organization/PC(USA)]]
- **Current Position:** Pastor, Good Shepherd Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (July 2019–Present)

## Education and Background

- Graduated from **National Institute of the Arts** (國立藝專), majoring in broadcasting and television
- Converted to Christianity in 1982 at a spiritual retreat (靈修會)
- Former executive producer of television gospel programs in Taiwan
- Worked at **National Cheng Kung University** (國立成功大學) Aerospace Institute audio-visual center
- Worked at National Cheng Kung University Medical College audio-visual center
- Passed Taiwan civil service qualifications exam

## Ministry

Rev. Hsu became the full-time pastor of Good Shepherd Taiwanese Presbyterian Church in July 2019, leading the congregation's Taiwanese-language worship, discipleship programs, and community outreach in the San Gabriel Valley Taiwanese American community.
