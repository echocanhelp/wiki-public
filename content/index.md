# Taiwanese American Historical Society

Welcome to the **Taiwanese American Historical Society** knowledge base — a living archive dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich history, culture, and contributions of Taiwanese Americans.

## Explore

- **[Taiwanese American History](history-taiwanese-americans)** — From the late 19th century to the present, tracing immigration waves, political shifts, and community milestones.
- **[Notable Taiwanese Americans](notable-taiwanese-americans)** — Profiles of Taiwanese Americans in politics, science, business, arts, and athletics.
- **[Community Organizations](organizations-taiwanese-americans)** — Advocacy groups, professional associations, student organizations, and regional Taiwan Centers.
- **[Settlement Patterns](settlement-taiwanese-americans)** — Where Taiwanese Americans live, from Bay Area and Flushing to Chandler, Arizona.
- **[Socioeconomics](socioeconomics-taiwanese-americans)** — Education, income, employment, and demographic statistics.
- **[Taiwanese American Cuisine](cuisine-taiwanese-americans)** — How Taiwanese restaurateurs reshaped Chinese food in America, modern restaurants, and cookbooks.
- **[TAO Community Organizations](community-organizations-tao)** — Directory of organizations from taiwaneseamerican.org.

## About This Project

This wiki is part of the **Echo System** — a multi-agent research platform that collects, verifies, and organizes Taiwanese American historical content from public sources. All content is sourced and attributed.
