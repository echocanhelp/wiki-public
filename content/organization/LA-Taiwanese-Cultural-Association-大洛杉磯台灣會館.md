# LA Taiwanese Cultural Association (大洛杉磯台灣會館)

## Overview

The LA Taiwanese Cultural Association (大洛杉磯台灣會館) is a community center and cultural venue in the San Gabriel Valley area of Los Angeles, serving as a gathering place for Taiwanese Americans in Southern California.

The 會館 (cultural association/clubhouse) model is common in Taiwanese communities worldwide, providing a physical space for cultural events, meetings, and community organizing.

## Key Functions

- Community meeting space for Taiwanese American organizations
- Cultural events and celebrations
- Venue for political and civic gatherings
- Social hub for the Taiwanese American community
