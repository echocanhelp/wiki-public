# First Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (第一台灣基督長老教會)

**First Taiwanese Presbyterian Church** (第一台灣基督長老教會), abbreviated **FTPC**, is a Taiwanese Presbyterian congregation in Pasadena, California. The church serves the Taiwanese American community in the San Gabriel Valley with worship services in [[language/Taiwanese-language-台語|台語]], Mandarin, and English.

## Ministry and Activities

FTPC offers worship in three languages (Taiwanese, Mandarin, English), reflecting the multilingual nature of the Taiwanese American community.

**Elder Choir (松年詩班)** — Active choir with performances recorded and shared on YouTube ([FTPC USA](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCZLQIZfe9GZeOdpZlBHVNkA), 149+ subscribers). Notable performances include:
- Christmas Community Concert (聖誕社區音樂會) 2017: "平安暝、聖誕暝" and "歡喜主臨世界"
- Regular Sunday service music (演奏, 獻詩)

**Ministries (團契生活):** Youth Group (社青團契), Women's Fellowship (婦女團契), and other fellowship groups.

**Bible Reading Movement (讀經運動)** — Active participation in Scripture reading initiatives, with over 269 related posts on the church blog.

## Online Presence

The church maintains a blog at [ftpcusa.com](https://www.ftpcusa.com) with regular posts including:
- Sunday service recordings (YouTube embedded)
- News and announcements (消息與報告) — 275+ posts
- Musical performances (演奏, 獻詩)
- Holiday special services (復活節, 母親節, 聖誕節)
- Taiwanese Contemporary Hymns (台語現代詩歌)

## Affiliations

FTPC is part of the organization/NTPC (National Taiwanese Presbyterian Council), which unites Taiwanese Presbyterian churches across the United States under the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.). The church's pastor, Rev. 謝信光, also serves as NTPC Secretary.
