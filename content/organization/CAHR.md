# CAHR (Chinese Association for Human Rights)

## Overview

**Founded by:** [[Hsu-Wen-pin-許文彬]] (Hsu Wen-Pin)

CAHR (Chinese Association for Human Rights) was an advocacy organization founded by [[Hsu-Wen-pin-許文彬]] to advance human rights causes, particularly focusing on indigenous communities in Taiwan.

CAHR was part of the broader network of Taiwanese American civic and advocacy organizations in the 1980s. 許文彬 was a prominent figure in the Taiwanese American community, serving as president of the [[Community-Federation-社團聯合會]] (社團聯合會) in Los Angeles and later founding CAHR to advance human rights causes.
