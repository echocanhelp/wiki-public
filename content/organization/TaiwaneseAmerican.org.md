# TaiwaneseAmerican.org

**TaiwaneseAmerican.org** is a web portal site highlighting many of the interesting people, events and organizations that make up Taiwanese America. Founded in 2006, it is both a volunteer-driven website and a non-profit organization.

## Mission

To connect, inform and promote the people, events and organizations that represent the next generation of the Taiwanese American community.

## Vision

To create an allied community for Americans of Taiwanese heritage by intersecting the diverse paths and passions of all our people.

## About

TaiwaneseAmerican.org was founded in 2006 by Ho Chie (蔡浩哲). The site aims to connect and promote those who identify with the Taiwanese identity, heritage, or culture, establishing a niche within the broader Asian Pacific American and mainstream communities.

The organization highlights the unique experiences of Taiwanese Americans while preserving and archiving an evolving history and legacy.

## Programs and Content

The site covers a wide range of topics relevant to the Taiwanese American community:

- **Stories and Interviews** — Profiles of Taiwanese American individuals
- **Perspectives** — Opinion pieces and community commentary
- **Arts & Entertainment** — Coverage of Taiwanese American artists and creatives
- **Community Organizations** — Directory and profiles of Taiwanese American organizations
- **Food & Travel** — Taiwanese cuisine and travel content
- **Social Issues & Politics** — Analysis of issues affecting the community
- **Taiwanese American Memoir Book Club** — Annual book club featuring Taiwanese American memoirs
- **Creative Writing Prizes** — Writing competitions for Taiwanese American writers
- **Parenting Resources** — Content for Taiwanese American parents

## Notable Coverage

The site has covered numerous Taiwanese American figures and events, including:

- **Gene Luen Yang** — Award-winning comic artist of Taiwanese descent, author of *American Born Chinese*
- **Kelvin Yu** — Actor and former Taiwanese American Foundation (TAF) camper, known for *Master of None*
- **Warren Hsu Leonard** — Taiwan-born writer who contributed to Disney's *American Born Chinese* adaptation
- **Grace Lin** — Children's book author of Taiwanese descent

## Relationship with TAHS

[TaiwaneseAmerican.org](https://www.taiwaneseamerican.org) and the [[organization/TAHS-臺美人文史學會|Taiwanese American Historical Society (TAHS)]] are separate organizations with complementary missions. TaiwaneseAmerican.org focuses on contemporary community connections and storytelling, while TAHS focuses specifically on historical documentation and archival work.
