## History

### Founding
NATPA was founded on **February 16, 1980**, at the University of Chicago. The South California Chapter was established later as an Ad Hoc Committee was formed to organize the local chapter.

**Ad Hoc Committee:**
- 陳昭俊 (Chair)
- 簡錦標
- 何汝諧 (First President)
- 謝清志 (Secretary/Treasurer)
- 王秋森

**Founding Meeting:** December 14, 1985, at Lincoln Plaza Hotel, Monterey Park ("Little Taipei")

### Growth
The S Cal Chapter grew from 15 founding members to nearly 100 — becoming NATPA's **largest and most active chapter** among its 11 regional chapters.

### Community Context
At the time of the chapter's establishment (1985), there were **45 active Taiwanese associations/organizations** in the greater Los Angeles area.

### Publications
- **"S Cal Tribune" (南加學壇)** — Launched later, publishing weekly articles in 國際日報 and 太平洋時報
- **"Speakers Bureau"** — Established to promote academic discourse in the community
