# NTPC (美國基督長老教會全國台灣人教會聯合會)

The **National Taiwanese Presbyterian Council** (NTPC, 美國基督長老教會全國台灣人教會聯合會) is the national umbrella organization uniting Taiwanese Presbyterian churches across the United States under the [[organization/PC(USA)]].

## Basic Information

- **Full Name (EN):** National Taiwanese Presbyterian Council, Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.)
- **Full Name (ZH):** 美國基督長老教會全國台灣人教會聯合會
- **Abbreviation:** NTPC
- **Website:** [ntpc-usa.org](https://www.ntpc-usa.org)
- **Denomination:** Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.)
- **Founded:** c. 1974 (52nd annual meeting held in 2026)
- **Member Churches:** 16 (as of 2025)

## Mission

The NTPC's stated purpose is to unite all Taiwanese Presbyterian churches and believers across the United States to:
- Jointly spread the gospel (宣揚福音)
- Bear witness to God's love and truth (見證上帝的愛和真理)
- Promote racial, cultural, social, and economic harmony and equality (促進種族、文化、社會和經濟之和諧與平等)

## Regional Structure

NTPC is organized into five geographic regions:
- **Northeast (東北區)**
- **Southwest (西南區)**
- **Central (中原區)**
- **Southern (美南區)**
- **Northwest (西北區)**

## Annual Conferences

NTPC holds annual conferences with keynotes, workshops, and fellowship. Recent conferences:

- **52nd Annual Conference (2026):** April 24-26, 2026, at Atlanta Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (亞特蘭大台灣基督長老教會), Duluth, GA. Keynote by Rev. 馬約翰 on "Rooted in God — The Key to Joyful Service" (釘根於上帝 — 喜樂服事之鑰).
- **51st Annual Conference (2025):** May 2-4, 2025, at Grace Taiwanese Presbyterian Church (恩惠台灣基督長老教會), Houston, TX. Theme: "Collecting and Passing Down NTPC's Faith History" (NTPC信仰歷史的收集與傳承). Keynote by Rev. 陳南州 on Taiwanese American Christian identity and missionary calling.

## Membership

Annual dues: $100 for churches with under 50 weekly attendance; $300 for churches with over 100; larger churches requested to contribute more. Payments accepted by check to NTPC or via Zelle.
